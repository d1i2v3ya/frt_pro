About the disease

Coronavirus Disease Outbreak Situation

COVID-19, caused by the virus SARS-CoV-2, is a respiratory illness that can range from mild to severe. It spreads through respiratory droplets when infected people cough or sneeze. Vaccination is the best defense against serious illness. Common symptoms include fever, cough, and shortness of breath. While most recover without medical attention, older adults and those with underlying conditions are at higher risk of complications. Practice good hygiene, wear a mask indoors, and get vaccinated to help protect yourself and others.

  • Avoid touching your face.
  • Cover your mouth and nose when coughing.
  • Stay home if you feel unwell.
How to Protect Yourself?

Prevention & advice

Stopping the spread of COVID-19 is a team effort. Protect yourself and others by staying up-to-date on vaccinations, wearing a mask indoors, practicing good hand hygiene, and maintaining distance in crowded spaces. If you're feeling unwell, isolate yourself and get tested. By working together, we can stay safe and healthy.
